DESCRIPTION
FOX PETERSON is a SPATIAL INFORMATICS ENGINEER based in the PORTLAND AREA.
While working for Oregon State University as a DOCTORAL RESEARCH ASSOCIATE, FOX developed a new method for estimating carbon sinks and fluxes using LIDAR scans, BIG DATA forest inventory records, GIS, and DISTRIBUTED NETWORKED SENSOR data.
As a POSTDOCTORAL RESEARCH ASSOCIATE in biomicrometeorology, FOX worked on eddy covariance towers, where she learned how to maintain and repair sensor equipment including mechanical and electrical components. Fox and her mentor published research on the COMPLEX effects of terrain and solar insolation on atmospheric carbon balance. This data is used to parameterize global climate models, including those that won the 2014 Nobel Prize, or on a smaller scale, to validate other atmospheric gas concentration studies.
FOX served the United States Forest Service as a DATA MANAGER in Corvallis, OR and worked with the Earth Science Informatics Partners and the Long Term Ecological Research program where excelled in bringing progressive, functional, distributed, graphically-driven web technology to scientists who had great ideas and data sets but little ability to develop tools to interact with them dynamically.
Most recently, FOX was invited to work at ENLIGHTED INC in Silicon Valley by the CEO at the time. She served directly under the CTO as 27th employee of a start-up lighting / IoT / energy management company. Agile development, bug-tracking, continuous integration, and other productivity skills were gained in a high-risk, high-reward business environment. Fox improved Enlighted's floorplan-based data and user interfaces by creating tools for monitoring long term data, optimizing the map-based workflow, and analysing the interpolation of fixture-based data over heterogeneous building spaces and occupancy patterns. After 2.5 years the ENLIGHTED INC was purchased by SIEMENS BUILDING TECHNOLOGIES . Fox was promoted to SENIOR RESEARCH SCIENTIST.
Fox left ENLIGHTED INC in good standing and now lives with her husband of over 10 years in VANCOUVER, WA.
